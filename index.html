Founder Bio

Harrison

My name is Harrison, and I've spent nearly 17 years in the food industry. Over the last eight years, I've served as the Executive Chef for a boutique film catering company and have also worked as a personal chef for a number of high-profile clients. Those experiences sharpened my standards, discipline, and respect for food as both fuel and craft.

My connection to food is personal. Earlier in my life, I struggled with overeating, lack of movement, and an unhealthy relationship with what I put on my plate. I was severely overweight, and changing that required real dietary maintenance, lifestyle shifts, and consistency—not quick fixes.

That journey reshaped how I cook and why I do it. I believe food should nourish, satisfy, and support performance without sacrificing enjoyment. Clean eating doesn't have to be boring, restrictive, or low-volume. You can eat well, feel full, and still genuinely enjoy your meals.

"Wolfpack Provisions was built to reflect that belief—real food, made with intention, designed to fuel people who want more out of their health and their lives."
